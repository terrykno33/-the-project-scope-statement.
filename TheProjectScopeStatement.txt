<h1>Tiny House Construction Project</h1>

<h2>Purpose of the Project</h2>
<p>
The purpose of this project is to design and construct a fully functional, code-compliant tiny house.
</p>

<h2>Scope</h2>

<h3>In Scope</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Architectural floorplans</li>
    <li>Foundation work</li>
    <li>Framing</li>
    <li>Roof installation</li>
    <li>MEP rough-in</li>
</ul>

<h3>Out of Scope</h3>
<ul>
    <li>Interior decoration</li>
    <li>Landscaping</li>
    <li>Smart systems</li>
</ul>

<h2>Deliverables</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Approved design</li>
    <li>Completed foundation</li>
    <li>Framed structure</li>
    <li>Installed roof</li>
    <li>MEP system</li>
</ul>

<h2>Budget</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Design: $800</li>
    <li>Foundation: $5,500</li>
    <li>Framing: $2,000</li>
    <li>Roofing: $2,500</li>
    <li>Siding: $2,300</li>
    <li>MEP: $6,000</li>
    <li>Inspection: $1,200</li>
</ul>
